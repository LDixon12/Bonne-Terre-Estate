Bonne Terre Estate

Bonne Terre Estate (website under construction) Is a new, upcoming event and wedding venue!
With the capacity of 130 people, Bonne Terre Estate Established in 2015 is the perfect place for a wedding or corporate event.
Located just off US highway 63, this 19 acres event space offers privacy while still maintaining a short to drive to downtown and other amenities.

Bonne Terre Estate is within 10 miles of downtown Columbia! Within that 10 miles you also have access to 8 hotels and 49 bars, cafes or restaurants.
For those out of town guests, we are just 4 miles from the Columbia Regional airport and 132 miles from the St. Louis Metro-East Airport.

Tools used in this project

  • MacOS 10.15.7
  • QGIS 3.14
    Created point feature Geojson of Bonne Terre Estate buildings for labeling purposes.
  • Data is shown on Mapbox web map with geolocation services enabled.
  • Data from OpenStreetMap:

    Amenities- filtered for bars, cafes, and restaurants within 5 and 10 miles of Bonne Terre Estate.

    Building- filtered for hotels withing 5 and 10 miles of Bonne Terre Estate.

    Aeroways- filterd for Columbia Regional Airport and St. Louis Metro-East Airport.
